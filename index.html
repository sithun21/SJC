DUTY FIRST - SINCE 1898

St. Joseph’s College is a leading national boys’ school in the North Central Province of Sri Lanka, well known for academic excellence, discipline, character development, and spiritual values. Our mission is to inspire young Josephians to become responsible, creative, and confident individuals who contribute positively to their community and nation. We offer quality education from Grade 1 to Grade 13 with modern facilities, dedicated teachers, and a learning environment built on the values of integrity, respect, and commitment.
