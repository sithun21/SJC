About Our College

Established in 1898, St. Joseph’s College is one of the oldest and most respected educational institutions in Anuradhapura.

With a proud history of more than a century, the school continues to serve the nation by producing talented, disciplined, and responsible young leaders.

The school provides education in both Sinhala and English mediums, supported by a professional teaching staff and modern infrastructure such as laboratories, the library, ICT facilities, sports grounds, and a swimming pool.
